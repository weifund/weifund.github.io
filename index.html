What is Weifund?


A Brief Overview

WeiFund is an open platform for crowdfunding campaigns. You can launch a campaign using one of Weifundâ€™s secure contract templates that we develop, or you can integrate your own campaign. To use the WeiFund decentralized app (dApp), users will first open WeiFund in a web 3.0 enabled browser such as Ethereum's Mist. A user would then immediately be able to start, contribute to, browse, and manage campaigns. WeiFund's interface and user experience will be very similar to that of conventional crowdfunding platforms such as Kickstarter or GoFundMe, however, all funds raised on WeiFund will be accounted for in the Ether digital-currency. Web 3.0 enabled browsers will come with their own wallet systems so that payments made on WeiFund to start and contribute to campaigns are done so in a secure and verifiable way.

Problems with Crowdfunding Today

Inaccessible

Campaigns are often regionally locked, making it inaccessible to the global market.

Slow

It could take up to xxx weeks before the campaigner receives anything.

Insecure

Blockchain-based crowdfunds are susceptible to hacks with unproven contracts.
